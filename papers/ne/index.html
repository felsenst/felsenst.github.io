Files in this folder



This directory contains the text of

    Felsenstein, J.  1992.  Estimating effective population size from
       samples of sequences: inefficiency of pairwise and segregating sites
       as compared to phylogenetic estimates.  Genetical Research  59: 139-147.

It is available as three files:
       
        ne.tex               The LaTeX source
        ne.ps                a Postscript version
        ne.pdf               a PDF version (without figures)

The figures for this paper are not available in computer-readable form.
