I. Michael Lerner

Quantitative geneticist working with poultry. Author of the books Genetic Homeostasis, Population Genetics and Animal Improvement, and The Genetic Basis of Selection. Influential in translating and publishing in English Zhores Medvedev's book on Lysenkoism. For many years professor at the University of California, Berkeley.

An obituary at the site of the Faculty Senate at the University of California.
