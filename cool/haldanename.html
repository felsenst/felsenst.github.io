J. B. S. Haldane

Human geneticist, evolutionary biologist, one of the three founders of theoretical population genetics (and thus of the modern evolutionary synthesis). He also was a pioneering researcher on the physiology of diving (following his father, who was the one who worked out the phenomenon of the "bends"). He was also a major scientific popularizer and generally outrageous character.
