Antônio Rodrigues Cordeiro

(I am a bit uncertain whether I have correctly labeled Cordeiro and Scossiroli).

Brazilian Drosophila geneticist who was a protegé of Dobzhansky. He was until his retirement at the Federal University of Rio Grande, Brazil. In 2000 he was awarded the Brazilian Order of Scientific Merit.

His biographical page at the Brazilian Academy of Sciences (in Portugese).
