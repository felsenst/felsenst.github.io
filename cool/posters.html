Help judge posters at SMBE 2014

Are you a graduate student or postdoctoral fellow? And are you planning to attend the 2014 SMBE meeting in San Juan, Puerto Rico?

You can help me, and help everyone, by becoming a judge in the poster sessions. I am organizing the judging, and we will need many judges.

What you have to do:

  1. You will be assigned a poster session and a list of 30-40 posters (probably contiguously numbered so they are all near each other). You will be given a rating form on which you score each poster 1 to 5 (5 is highest).
  2. If you have a poster yourself, you will not be asked to do this in that poster session, as I know you need time to "stand" your poster.
  3. Each judge will do the task during one of the poster sessions, and turn the scores in to me. This should not interfere with any of your other activities at the meeting.
  4. Don't worry that you have too little experience to do this. We need judges of all levels of sophistication. One of the properties of a good poster is to communicate clearly to beginners, and we need people who can rate this.

Your participation is important to us. All I can offer in return is my gratitude plus buying you one drink, if you want.

If you can't do this, tell some friends about this wonderful opportunity. We need many more judges.
